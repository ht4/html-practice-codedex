Red Hot Chili Peppers

The Red Hot Chili Peppers, formed in 1983 in Los Angeles, are renowned for blending funk, punk, and psychedelic rock. Famous for hits like "Under the Bridge" and "Californication," they have won multiple Grammy Awards and are celebrated for their energetic live performances.
